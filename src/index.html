About Us

Cinema creations together with those who dream, support and invest in inspiring stories. We are a crowdfunding platform that unites dreamers, fans and investors who want to leave their mark in the world of Cinema. Here, everyone can become part of a great process - from idea to premiere.

Your story, your support, your faith can make a film a reality. We create a space where new names, new stories and new opportunities are born. Because Cinema is not the privilege of the chosen ones. Cinema is the power of community.

How It Works

1

Choose a Film

Browse our current projects and select one that interests you.

2

Fill the Form

Complete our application form with your details.

3

Sending a script for audition

We send you a part of the script with the chosen role.

4

Recording a video

You film a test at home and send it to us. The director reviews the video and, if approved, invites you to a reading with other actors.

5

Confirmation

After confirmation, we sign a contract, you book and pay for the role and officially become part of the film.

6

In case of an unsuccessful attempt

We can provide an acting teacher to help you achieve your dream.
